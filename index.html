Project Idea

Overview

My project is a medical app. This would include being able to store medical records as well as blood tests, keep track of appointments, a reminder for taking medication and for operations. It would also track your fitness and health over time in line with the medication if it impacts it. The main purpose would be to have an app that can hold all your medical-related stuff, so it is all in 1 app instead of having some physical documents go missing. You can also choose to share some or all that info with a family member or someone else as well as with your GP.

Motivation

As for as I know, I have not seen an app where it holds all medical records, or I just have not seen one yet. Also, both my parents go to the doctors often and have a blood test taken and they also both take medication. My dad came up with the idea where I should make an app that can store all your medical related stuff which can help a lot of people, whether they are in good health and do not go to the doctors much and especially for those who visit the doctors a lot.

Description

Medical records and blood tests/records - One of the main features of the app. You would be able to scan hard copy versions of medical records, like GP visits, hospital visits and other medical-related things. You would also be able to import digital medical records. Same as the medical records, you would be able to store digital copies and scan physical copies of blood tests and blood reports. Both can be sorted by whether it is from the GP or hospital, arranged according to date, which facility it was from specifically. You can also add your comments about the document. Appointments - You would be able to enter the appointments manually and it would look like a calendar. You could also import an appointment message and it can format it automatically the way the app wants it to. You can also set a reminder for when you want to be reminded about the appointment. You can also add comments to that appointment and there would be an option the make the appointment recurring, either every week or every 2 weeks. Medication - Another feature would be to keep track of your medication as well as have reminders. You can either manually type in or scan your medication form into the app. This will hold what medicine you are holding and keep track of any repeats you need to take. You can set daily reminders of when you want to take the medication and when to re-order your medication as well. You would also be able to categorise what the medication is for, e.g., you can group your basic Panadol and you can group your heart medication etc. Operations/Procedures - This feature would be for storing when you have an operation or procedure. This includes when the operation is, and any documents related to it. You can set a reminder for when you want to be notified and a reminder for when you can cancel the appointment at the latest. Health and Fitness - You would be able to enter your health and fitness details at the beginning. You can also add updates when you want or every week, you can then view your progress over time. This would mainly be for those whose medication and visits are healthrelated. It would show whether the medication and other activities your doing is helping with your health and fitness goals. Notifications - You can customise your notifications. By default, you would get notifications about everything: appointments, medication, procedures etc. You can also choose to send notifications to others as well by SMS or email. .
